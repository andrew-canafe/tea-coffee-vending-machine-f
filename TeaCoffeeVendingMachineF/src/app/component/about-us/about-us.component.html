About Us

We are a tea/coffee vending machine service provider that uses automated technology to detect when vending machine containers need to be refilled. Whenever an order is made for tea/coffee, a request is made to our backend server to update the relevant database records. If the records indicate that the containers need to be refilled, a notification is sent to nearby workers for automatic refilling. As a result, your business will not need to experience the hassle of managing vending machine operations. Instead, we handle these operations ourselves and take the full responsibility off of your shoulders.
